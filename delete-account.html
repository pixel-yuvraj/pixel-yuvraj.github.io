Delete Your FeedLove Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the FeedLove app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

What gets deleted: Your profile, email, name, profile photo, and all food listings you posted.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

If you face any issues, contact us at: privacy@feedlove.app
